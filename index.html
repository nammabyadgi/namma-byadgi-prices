AC COLD STORAGE (Lower moisture, more stable color & SHU)

What is AC Storage?

Chillies kept in cold storage after proper drying.

ASTA: 50-240 (Lower color) SHU: Same as New Crop Less color when powdered, lower market price

AC lots usually have **better shelf-life** and consistency across bags; they are preferred by spice brands that want stable color rather than very high brightness.

New Crop / Moisture Lots

Fresh arrivals with field or sun moisture.

ASTA: 120-260 (Higher color) SHU: Same as AC Storage Better color & taste, premium quality & price

Moisture lots give a **brighter colour** and stronger aroma after processing, but need expert drying and cleaning before grinding to avoid spoilage.
